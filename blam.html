About

Originally in 1999, developed as a Mac Sci-Fi RTS, the Blam! engine eventually would power the ancient, epic, and mysterious world of Halo: Combat Evolved. Later in 2003, Halo: Custom Edition released on PC would open the door for creative & passionate fans to create their own worlds within the engine.
